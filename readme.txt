A plugin that lets the user exchange customer traffic with other ecommerce shops that have this plugin.

=== Description ===

Run ads on other e-commerce stores. Drive traffic with high purchase intent to your website to generate more sales. 

Sixads is trusted by thousands of shops that run on Woocommerce, Shopify, Wix, and Weebly. The plugin enables you to exchange audiences from all of those platforms and reach ready-to-buy users instantly. 

Set up and have your ad campaign running in under 2 mins. Sixads comes with a FOREVER FREE or paid plan.You can also have your ad account reviewed by our in-house marketing gurus to get the best results possible.
 
=== How does it work? ===

1. Install Sixads plugin.
2. Set up your ads.
3. Adjust your settings and targeting.
4. Review your results. 
5. Start scaling your ads that work best. 

=== Main features ===

* Ad campaign manager. Create, customize and edit your ads as needed.

* Performance analytics. Monitor ad performance online to see your progress.

* Country targeting. Display your ads in countries where your target audience is. 

* Adjustable look and feel. Choose where to display partner products. Fine tune them to suit the style of your store.

* Easy integration. Simply install Sixads and get going right away. 

* Category targeting. Display your ads with products that fit your business strategy.

* No commitments. No credit card needed. Use Sixads as much or as little only when you need it. You can pause your campaigns anytime. 

=== Installation ===
 
1. Upload the entire `sixads` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory OR download the plugin from the official WP plugin store.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. On the admin bar click the field 'SixAds'. 
4. The user is redirected to the sixads server and the onboarding procedure is done there.
5. In the desired place of the page (category page, product page, etc) add the short tag **[sixads]**.

=== Paid services ===

Plugin comes with a forever free plan. 

Paid plans are for purchasing additional boosted impressions. Boosted impressions have higher CTR (click through rate) and help you hit your sales targets quicker. Default options for paid plans are 2 000, 5 000, 10 000 impressions, their prices start at $5.83.

=== Data usage ===

By agreeing with the terms and conditions of the SixAds plugin the user allows the app to pull data ONLY from the WooCommerce API. The data that is used is mostly about the shop, its items, webhooks and dates.
